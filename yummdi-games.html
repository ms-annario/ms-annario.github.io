📌 BACKGROUND
        This project arises from the problem of people who need to buy products but rarely use them, for example, when you think of “Big board game” - requires a large group of friends, “Barbecue griller” for the party. Sometimes, this could be a very expensive product, but we still need to buy it and make you feel worried 😰 about “its value”, “maintenance” and “your limited space”

        So, designing this experience to address this issue will be very helpful for the younger generation who don't have the time, the limited space but I want to join every activities extremely.

📌 Findings TO IDEATION!
        📓 I was on user interview with the example of users - 5 students, 5 first jobbers (aged 21-25), 5 workers (aged 26-45). We found many interesting key themes as follows.

        👉 'First jobbers' to 'Workers' (aged 21-45) is a great majority who used to face a problem with high product demand with a rare chance of use!
        👉 Over 67% of people live in condos with limited space and need more space to do activities, even in the people who live in a house faces these problems as well.
        👉 The high amount of products is included “Games”, “Comic books”, “Travel sweaters”, “Go Pro cameras” and etc.

        Moreover, we primarily have observed from many posts related to the projects. We found that this problem can be dealt with by "Exchange the use of goods" ex. Lending service
‍
       We, therefore, looked for statistical reports written by a professor who specialises in statistical data in Thailand. It was found that 72%, of aged 18 - 45, want to use second-hand products that have “Great functional” followed by "Cleanliness" (especially a concern of infection*).
‍

📌 Competitive analysis:

        We have searched for current applications that also meets the needs of the group over 20 worldwide platforms - lending service, second-hand shopping platforms, etc. We've found that they tend to focus on "Sustainability", "Reduce Global Warming", "Earnings".

‍

‍
        For the competitive analysis, we still have concerns in some points that we want to improve to be our strength as follows!

        1. "Cleanliness" because we cannot guarantee the quality of cleanliness from the lender. If we can seriously improve this part of experience. The product will be outstanding as "Best Maintenance 🔧, Best Cleanliness 🧽" that can cause the “scenario of “Customer can lend a new quality product”.
‍
        2. “Short term lending, the goods will be placed again in the same room corner” If we can make the borrowing is a long term investment (Like a pile of funds), it will not clutter the house. and keep earn some passive income from it.

        Apart from that, we did a business canvas by designed on Mural board to support our key activities then, Lets' ideate!
‍
        So, we grabbed these concepts by beginning with "Games" - a majority product, where we truly have roles as experts in cleaning and maintenance. We didn't hesitate to take the first step from "Games".‍

📌 FINAL SOLUTION
         “Yummdi”, /Yumm means 'borrow' in thai/, a product investing platform that gives everyone the opportunity to share games (of all kinds) to a central warehouse. Then, get income from your own product continuously.
1. Become our partner
START INVESTING ON your product
Users can bring rarely used product and left with us. we will evaluate, clean, then be ready to send to interested customers. you will receive passive income continuously according to the demand that arises.
2. cleanliness
Guaranteed BY OUR PROFESSIONALs
Every item in the central warehouse will go through cleaning and maintenance by experts. Users will feel great open the box that patched with the cleaning confirmation from our staffs.
3. Easy to start borrow
LET'S BE OUR MEMBERSHIP
Our system will make the membership application easy, no hassle to let the customer send a lot of evidence. You can order anytime, delivered to the front of the house.
4. Rate the customer ⭐️⭐️⭐️
What will guarantee the quality of treatment of users? Well, what's the score! When you get a good score. we would love to give many promotions for borrowing the next products.
5. Blockchain SYSTEM ⛓
We will use the Blockchain system to track the borrower information so that we know the exact borrowing history and this information can be extended a lot of business opportunity.
