📌 About "My Careers Future"
        MyCareersFuture is a portal that aims to provide Singapore Citizens and Permanent Residents with a fast and smart job search service to match them with relevant jobs, based on their skills and competencies which was developed by Workforce Singapore , in partnership withthe Government Technology Agency.
📌 Client Background
        GovTech, or Government Technology Agency of Singapore, harnesses the best info-communications technologies to build a Smart Nation & Digital Government their work can be broadly classified into three areas which is “Products”, “Services”, “Cybersecurity & Governance”.
        MyCareersFuture which is categorized as “Product” has around 30 tech-talented employees incorporating 3 teams which are “Jobseeker”, “Employment” and “CareersFinder” team.
        While MyCareersFuture was planning to release the new feature “CareersFinder”, they lacked a person who has a capability to work on Visual Design. Moreover, with a lot of assigned tasks, they have limited designer so that they came directly to us to find designer who can assist and consult them on their overall “UI Improvement”
📌 Why me?
        I was selected as the best candidate among 8 software agencies. From proving that not only driving value through extraordinary customer experiences with proven design methodologies, but also pushing the unlimited visual design solution.
📌 CHALLENGE
👉 Challenge:
     Design for solving the problem of people who need to buy some product but rarely or no longer used.

🖊  Role:     
     Experience Designer, Interface Designer, Animator

🔧 Tools:    
     Figma, Framer, Illustrator, After Effect, Photoshop

⏰ Project duration:     
     2.8 months (16 January 2023 - 31 March 2023)
‍
💼 Stakeholders:     
     
        I collaborated closely with User Experience Designers - from CareersFinder, Job Seeker, and Employer team - Product Owners, and Delivery Managers in every internal design discussion which happened 3 times weekly.
        Then, followed by the Head of Product Owner in ‘Weekly Design Sharing’, in which all designers have to present their design updates to the rest of the team. Sometimes, included related stakeholders from another segment of GovTech for example, Workforce Singapore team, Subject matter experts, and compliance.
        More than the design part, we also collaborated closely with developers to ensure overall experience and the illustrative design quality on MCF platform.
📌 PROCESS
1. Research
2. Define
3. Ideation & Create business design

        I brought experience and visual design improvements to every aspect of the MyCareersFuture portal - incorporating Job Seekers, Employers, and CareersFinder, the new feature which I mainly focused on creating visual design and branding, while the other parts already have a quite stable branding and design system.
        After acknowledging their obstacles. They gave us the opportunity to create a development plan independently so I proudly proposed a “UI Design development plan” which consisted of 3 types of tasks.
1. In-development Plan (70%) for “CareersFinder”, I created this plan arising from our own design audit where we planned everything on a clear timeline.

📌 Challenges
1. ‘Learning’ and ‘Adapting’ to the new context in a short time is always challenging, for example immersing yourself, starting with understanding the government office’s mission,vision of the product, the new working system, structures, acronyms. So, having some discussions with stakeholders or any preparation is needed before working on-site.
2. I had such a great opportunity to work with over 30 customers, So, it is essential to have “adaptive” skills - in understanding their working process, moreover, being empathetic with whoever we worked with.
3. I managed 3 different features in the meantime. Therefore, we should have clear conversations with the team to prioritize each task before starting.
4. Any alteration of any long-time existing design. it is neccesity to show high sincerity and authenticity in transforming sincerely with our “drives great outcome” mindset and prove articulately why our solution is appropriate for you.
📌 Outcomes
1. I delivered quality-pleasant experience and visual design to the customers which completely met their expectations on-time with our agreement in the first place, moreover, we also planned for the future of the product that customers got inspired by many possibilities of ideas which can be implemented on post MVP.
2. Company has been successful building trust with GovTech (MyCareersFuture) which they want to collaborate with us again in the future furthur.
